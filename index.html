Passion for Design ∼ Passion for Life!

My Story!

Nur Yilmaz Ruppi, a Turkish-Italian architect and interior designer, embraces a cosmopolitan identity, drawing inspiration from her diverse cultural background. Born in Istanbul and raised in Tekirdag, her passion for decor and interior design emerged in her childhood years.

After architectural studies in Istanbul, she gained experience in London and Florence before relocating to Barcelona with her husband. In Spain, she contributed to hotel projects and worked for a Turkish furniture company.

Later, in Brussels, Nur raised her family while continuing to write for interior design magazines. Her deep-rooted passion is the key driving force which enhances her love for her work.

Settling in the tranquil town of Vicenza, Italy, she finds inspiration in daily pursuits, travels, and the calming sound of the waves at the seaside. Nur dreams of travelling in a camper with her family, reminiscent of her childhood adventures.

Nur Yilmaz Ruppi exceeds being just an architect and designer; she is a global citizen whose diverse experiences enrich her creative endeavours.

Every design begins with your dream, make your dream a reality!

Nur's Philosophy!

Nur creates interiors where people feel a profound sense of relaxation and comfort, seamlessly blending aesthetics, functionality, and beauty.

Guided by a holistic work philosophy, she approaches her projects from every angle. Meticulously analysing her clients' lifestyles, she designs and creates ambient environments tailored to both their workspaces and living areas. Her projects are environmentally friendly, with a focus on using mostly natural materials, artisan fabrics and art objects that enhance her clients' well-being.

Her passion for design is deeply rooted in a love for diverse cultures and art, shaping her dedication to producing distinctive, culturally-informed designs that set her work apart.

What is Upcycle?

Upcycling is a transformative and eco-friendly practice that revitalises discarded or unwanted items, breathing new life into them to elevate their value.

This creative repurposing of materials encourages innovative thinking, leading to aesthetically pleasing solutions such as converting old furniture into stylish pieces or crafting unique decor.

Upcycling contributes to a more sustainable lifestyle by transforming the old into something new and valuable.

Reasons for Upcycling! ∼ Nur's Concept!

Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication

∼ Leonardo Da Vinci

Repairing is Respecting

Why recycle when you can refashion cherished materials, objects and furniture?
Upcycling uses existing materials to give your belongings a second life, whereas recycling breaks them down to make new consumer products, using only a fraction of the original piece.

The Environment is Our Responsibility

We already own cherished pieces. Let Nur bring new life to them within your home.
With this objective and philosophy at the forefront, she works with her clients to revamp existing furniture during the renovation of their space.

Old and Used?

Definitely not. History, yes. The satisfaction Nur derives from giving new life to furniture and spaces is a therapeutic loop that she wishes to share with her clients.
Sustainability is the future of our planet.
Let’s explore the artistic and environmental value of upcycling in your beautiful home!
